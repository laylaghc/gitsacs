Technical Support

Navigating the GHC SACS USB Flash Drive

To effectively read and navigate within this electronic version of the Compliance Audit, you will need the following:

  • Personal computer with a USB port
  • A standards compliant web browser (Chrome, Firefox, Internet Explorer, or Safari)
  • PDF document viewer (Adobe Acrobat Reader, Chrome, or Firefox)
  • An Internet connection (Optional)


Navigation Notes
The main page of this documentation is accessed by navigating to the root of your USB flash drive and clicking the file named "index.html". A horizontal menu across the header of this page will allow you to navigate the available areas of content.

All essential files are written onto the USB flash drive, however, if you encounter secondary links to off-site URLs, you will need an active Internet connection.


PDF Document Viewer
If you require Adobe Acrobat Reader, you may install the software from the web address https://get.adobe.com/reader.

Georgia Highlands College is a multi-campus, state college member of the University System of Georgia. Founded in 1970 as Floyd Junior College, the college now serves thousands of students from over 30 counties in Northwest Georgia. GHC has five sites in Rome, Cartersville, Marietta, and Dallas, as well as a robust online program. GHC offers over 40 areas of study with associate degree and bachelor's degree options both in the classroom and online. GHC is proud to support the local economy with a regional economic impact of nearly $170 million.
